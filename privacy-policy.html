Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 September 2025

At GenArt, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our website, services, and products (collectively, “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We use your information to provide and improve our services, manage your account, process payments, communicate with you, and ensure platform security.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information and opt-out of marketing communications. Contact us at team@genart.space to exercise these rights.

We use cookies to remember preferences, track performance, and support authentication. You can disable cookies in your browser.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. However, no method is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of our Services means you accept any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at team@genart.space.
