Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as one credit?

One credit allows you to generate one image. If you generate a batch of four images, it will use four credits.

Do my credits expire?

It depends on your plan. The 'Create' plan credits expire in 3 months, and 'Elevate' in 5 months. Credits from the 'Pro' plan never expire.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the computational costs of AI generation, we do not offer refunds on credit purchases. All sales are final.
